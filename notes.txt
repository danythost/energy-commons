Energy Commons Project Overview
==============================
1. Energy Commons is a decentralized platform for community energy management.
2. It empowers local residents to monitor and report the state of their power grid.
3. The application uses a zone-based system to organize community energy data.
4. Each zone is managed by a Steward who validates energy reports from users.
5. Users can report whether their power is currently ON or OFF in real-time.
6. This social reporting helps build a reliable map of power availability.
7. The platform integrates blockchain technology using the Cardano network.
8. Users can purchase Power Units using ADA, Energy Tokens, or Bank Transfers.
9. Energy Steward Tokens (EST) reward active participation and problem reporting.
10. Power Access Tokens (PAT) are granted as bonuses for ADA-based purchases.
11. A dedicated Buy page simplifies the process of topping up energy credits.
12. The Steward Dashboard provides tools for user validation and zone statistics.
13. Stewards can gift tokens to users for helpful community contributions.
14. The system features a mobile-first design for easy access on the go.
15. Real-time updates ensure the community is always informed of grid status.
16. Future updates include NFT minting for energy certificates and impact badges.
17. The project aims to improve energy transparency and local accountability.
18. It bridges the gap between traditional power monitoring and digital assets.
19. Built with Laravel, Alpine.js, and Tailwind CSS for a premium experience.
20. Energy Commons: Empowering communities through collective energy insights.
